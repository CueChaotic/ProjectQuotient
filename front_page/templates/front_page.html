About us

We're a group of gamers, here to make games. We weren't too happy with the quality of the stuff being put out there, so we wanted to see if we could do better. We wanted to craft games centered around strong and well-developed narratives, characters that you love and love to hate, exploring from the deepest oceans to the furthest frontiers of the Milky Way. Not only do we want you to be able to witness clashing empires across the course of human history, on Earth and worlds afar; but also explore the stories and journeys of characters that you can grow fond of.

History isn't just made here, it's forged to perfection.
